## Features

- **Task list** — colour-coded categories (Work, Personal, Learning, Urgent and more) with per-task focus durations
- **Focus timer** — customisable sessions from 5–25 minutes with Live Activities on Dynamic Island and Lock Screen
- **Weekly targets** — set goals per category and track overall progress with visual progress bars
- **Statistics** — total focus time, tasks/day average, and productivity heatmap showing your most active hours
- **Daily streaks** — stay motivated with current and longest streak tracking
- **AI task splitting** — powered by Apple Intelligence (iOS 26+), splits complex tasks into subtasks automatically
- **Repeating tasks** — set tasks to recur automatically
- Available on iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch and Mac
